## What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting asks the AI to solve a problem step by step before providing the final answer. This improves reasoning, transparency, and accuracy, especially for complex tasks like planning, coding, and career guidance.

### Example

**Normal Prompt**
> What is 15% of 240?

**Answer**
> 36

**Chain-of-Thought Prompt**
> Calculate 15% of 240 and explain each step.

**Answer**
1. Convert 15% to decimal = 0.15
2. Multiply 240 × 0.15
3. Final Answer = 36
