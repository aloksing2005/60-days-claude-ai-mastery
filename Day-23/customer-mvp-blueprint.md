# Customer & MVP Blueprint

# Executive Summary

The AI-Powered Hospital Management System aims to simplify healthcare operations by offering cloud-based patient management, appointment scheduling, billing, AI-assisted clinical support, pharmacy, laboratory integration, and analytics.

The priority is to build an MVP that solves the most urgent operational challenges before expanding with advanced AI capabilities.

---

# Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)

## ICP 1 – Small Clinics

- 1–10 doctors
- Limited IT infrastructure
- Need affordable digital management
- Prefer cloud-based software

## ICP 2 – Medium Hospitals

- 20–100 beds
- Multiple departments
- Require integrated patient and billing systems
- Interested in analytics and reporting

## ICP 3 – Diagnostic Centers

- High volume of lab reports
- Need report management and online access
- Require integration with hospitals and clinics
